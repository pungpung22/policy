Market Companion does not currently require an account. App preferences and unlock states are stored locally on your device.

Delete Local App Data

You can remove locally stored Market Companion data by clearing app data where supported or uninstalling the app from your device. Generated PDF files shared or saved outside the app must be deleted from the location where you saved them.

Advertising Data

Advertising-related information may be processed by Google AdMob according to Google's policies. You can manage iOS tracking permissions in Settings and manage advertising-related settings through your device and Google settings where available.
