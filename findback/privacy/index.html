FindBack is a link saving and search app for iPhone, iPad, and Mac. This Privacy Policy explains what information may be processed when you use FindBack.

Information We Process

Account Information

If you create an account or sign in, we may process your email address, user ID, workspace ID, account creation time, session cookies, and password hash. We do not store your password in plain text.

Saved Links and Notes

When you save a link, FindBack may process the original URL, normalized URL, page title, source domain, content type, notes you enter, generated categories, tags, topics, keywords, saved date, and basic item metadata needed to show, search, open, and delete saved links.

Metadata Fetching

FindBack may fetch public HTML metadata from a saved URL, such as title, description, and site name, to make saved links easier to recognize. FindBack is designed to skip local, private, and unsafe network targets during server-side metadata fetching.

Cookies and Local App Data

FindBack may use signed cookies to maintain account sessions and guest workspaces. The apps may store settings, API connection settings, temporary share extension data, and local UI state on your device.

Technical Data

For security, abuse prevention, diagnostics, and service operation, our API host may process technical information such as IP address, request time, API path, response status, app version, OS version, client type, error logs, and rate-limit information.

How We Use Information

  • To create accounts, sign users in, and maintain sessions.
  • To save, sync, search, open, and delete links.
  • To move guest saves into an account when requested.
  • To support iOS Share Extension saving.
  • To extract link metadata and improve search quality.
  • To protect the service, prevent abuse, and diagnose problems.
  • To comply with legal, security, and platform requirements.

Third-Party Services

FindBack may rely on Apple for app distribution and operating system features, Supabase or another hosted Postgres provider for database storage, Railway or another API hosting provider for the FindBack API, and infrastructure providers for networking and deployment.

FindBack does not include third-party advertising SDKs, third-party analytics SDKs, or tracking SDKs in the current release. FindBack does not use saved link data to track you across other companies' apps or websites.

AI Processing

External AI processing is off by default in the current release. If AI features are added in the future, this policy and App Store privacy disclosures will be updated as needed.

Retention and Deletion

Account information and saved links may be stored while your account remains active or while you keep the saved items. If you delete a saved item, FindBack deletes that item from the service storage. If you delete your account, FindBack deletes account data and linked saved items, except for limited records that may be retained for security, legal compliance, fraud prevention, backups, or dispute handling.

Your Choices

  • Delete saved links in the app.
  • Log out of your account.
  • Delete your account from the account screen or request help by email.
  • Delete the app to remove local app data from your device, subject to operating system backup settings.
  • Contact us to request access, correction, deletion, or other privacy help.

Children

FindBack is not directed to children under 13 and is not intended to collect personal information from children.

Security

FindBack uses password hashing, signed cookies, production secrets, rate limiting, security headers, and metadata-fetch safeguards. No internet service can guarantee perfect security, but we work to protect user data with reasonable safeguards.

Changes

We may update this policy when FindBack changes, when our infrastructure changes, or when legal or platform requirements change. The updated policy will be posted on this page.
