Delete Your Account in the App

If you are signed in, open the account sync section in FindBack and choose Delete Account. Confirming account deletion removes your account and saved link data associated with that account, except for limited records that may be retained for legal, security, fraud prevention, backup, or dispute handling purposes.

What Is Deleted

  • Your account email address, user ID, and password hash.
  • Your account workspace ID.
  • Saved links connected to your account.
  • Saved URLs, notes, titles, metadata, tags, categories, summaries, keywords, and related item metadata.

What May Be Retained Temporarily

Backups, security logs, abuse-prevention records, and technical logs may be retained for a limited period when necessary for service operation, security, legal compliance, fraud prevention, or dispute handling.

If You Cannot Access the App

Email pung0805@gmail.com from the email address associated with your account and request account deletion. We may ask for information needed to verify account ownership before completing the request.
