Overview Privacy Policy Support Terms

Current Native App

The current native FindBack app is designed to sync saved links through your private iCloud database and does not require a separate FindBack account for bookmark sync. You can delete saved links in the app. You can manage iCloud sign-in and app data through Apple system settings.

Previous Account-Based Builds

If you used a previous or test build with a FindBack account, open the account sync section in that build and choose Delete Account, or contact support by email. Confirming account deletion removes your account and saved link data associated with that account, except for limited records that may be retained for legal, security, fraud prevention, backup, or dispute handling purposes.

What Is Deleted

  • Saved links that you delete in the current native app.
  • FindBack account email address, user ID, password hash, and workspace ID if you used an account-based build.
  • Saved links connected to a previous FindBack account, if applicable.
  • Saved URLs, notes, titles, metadata, tags, categories, summaries, keywords, and related item metadata.

What May Be Retained Temporarily

Backups, security logs, abuse-prevention records, and technical logs may be retained for a limited period when necessary for service operation, security, legal compliance, fraud prevention, or dispute handling.

If You Cannot Access the App

Email pung0805@gmail.com and request account or data deletion help. If your request relates to an older account-based build, email from the address associated with your account when possible. We may ask for information needed to verify account ownership before completing the request.
