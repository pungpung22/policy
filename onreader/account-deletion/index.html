Current Account Model

OnReader can use Sign in with Apple for optional identity features, but the core library is stored locally on your device. Deleting the app removes local OnReader app data from the device, subject to iOS backup and restore settings.

How to Delete or Disconnect

  • Delete imported files and library items inside OnReader when available.
  • Delete OnReader from iOS to remove local app data from the device.
  • Open Apple ID settings to stop using Sign in with Apple for OnReader.
  • Email pung0805@gmail.com if you need help with deletion or privacy requests.

Data That May Remain

Some limited records may remain where required for security, legal compliance, fraud prevention, billing, backups, crash diagnostics, or platform requirements. Data processed by Apple, Firebase, AdMob, AI providers, or other third-party services is subject to their own retention policies.
