Superball is a lottery strategy utility for generating, comparing, and saving number picks for Powerball and Mega Millions. This Privacy Policy explains how information may be stored or processed when you use the app.

Superball is informational only. It does not sell lottery tickets, process gambling transactions, or claim that generated numbers improve your odds of winning. Superball is independent and is not affiliated with Powerball, Mega Millions, or any lottery operator.

Information Stored on Your Device

Superball can store app data locally on your device so the app can remember your experience. This may include:

  • Saved number picks.
  • Selected lottery type, such as Powerball or Mega Millions.
  • Generated pick details, strategy name, lucky score, and short analysis text.
  • App settings and local unlock states related to rewarded ad flows.
  • Local result matching information used to compare saved picks with displayed winning numbers.

This local app data is not sent to PungPung Studio by default.

No Account Required

Superball does not currently require users to create an account. We do not ask for your name, email address, password, payment card, or government identifier inside the app.

Lottery and Pick Information

Generated picks and saved picks are used to provide the app's strategy, save, and match-check features. Generated numbers are for informational and entertainment use only. The app does not submit entries to any lottery operator.

Advertising

Superball uses Google AdMob to show ads. The default production app uses opt-in rewarded ads to unlock additional picks, strategy batches, or detailed analysis. An interstitial placement may be enabled in a future compliant release or configuration.

Google AdMob may process information such as advertising identifiers, device and app information, IP address, approximate location, ad impressions, ad interactions, consent status, and diagnostic information for ad delivery, frequency capping, fraud prevention, measurement, and personalization where allowed.

The default production app requests non-personalized ads and does not request App Tracking Transparency permission. If personalized advertising or tracking is added in a future version, iOS may show Apple's App Tracking Transparency permission prompt. You can manage tracking permissions in iOS Settings.

Notifications

Superball may include optional draw reminder or notification features. If you enable notifications, Apple Push Notification service may process the device token needed to deliver notifications. Notification preferences may be stored locally on your device.

Diagnostics and Remote Configuration

Superball uses Firebase Crashlytics to help diagnose crashes and improve app stability. Crash reports may include crash logs, device and app information, operating system version, timestamps, installation identifiers, and diagnostic details related to the crash.

Superball also uses Firebase Remote Config to manage app settings such as feature availability, ad placement settings, and unlock limits. Remote configuration may process app and device information, installation identifiers, IP address, locale, timestamps, and configuration fetch information.

We use this information to operate, secure, debug, and improve the app. We do not use Crashlytics or Remote Config to sell lottery tickets, process wagers, or identify individual lottery activity.

Third-Party Services

Superball may use third-party services including Apple platform services, Google AdMob, Firebase Crashlytics, and Firebase Remote Config. These services may process information according to their own privacy policies and terms.

Data Deletion

You can delete locally stored Superball data by deleting saved picks inside the app, clearing app data where supported, or uninstalling the app from your device. For privacy questions, contact pung0805@gmail.com.

Children's Privacy

Superball is not directed to children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information to us, contact us so we can review and take appropriate action.

Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy when app features, advertising behavior, third-party services, legal requirements, or platform policies change. Updates will be posted on this page with a new effective date.
