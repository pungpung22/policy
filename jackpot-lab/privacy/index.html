Jackpot Lab is a lottery strategy utility for generating, comparing, and saving number picks for Powerball and Mega Millions. This Privacy Policy explains how information may be stored or processed when you use the app.

Jackpot Lab is informational only. It does not sell lottery tickets, process gambling transactions, or claim that generated numbers improve your odds of winning.

Information Stored on Your Device

Jackpot Lab can store app data locally on your device so the app can remember your experience. This may include:

  • Saved number picks.
  • Selected lottery type, such as Powerball or Mega Millions.
  • Generated pick details, strategy name, lucky score, and short analysis text.
  • App settings and local unlock states related to rewarded ad flows.
  • Local result matching information used to compare saved picks with displayed winning numbers.

This local app data is not sent to PungPung Studio by default.

No Account Required

Jackpot Lab does not currently require users to create an account. We do not ask for your name, email address, password, payment card, or government identifier inside the app.

Lottery and Pick Information

Generated picks and saved picks are used to provide the app's strategy, save, and match-check features. Generated numbers are for informational and entertainment use only. The app does not submit entries to any lottery operator.

Advertising

Jackpot Lab uses Google AdMob to show ads. This may include interstitial ads and rewarded ads. Rewarded ads may unlock additional picks, strategy batches, or detailed analysis inside the app.

Google AdMob may process information such as advertising identifiers, device and app information, IP address, approximate location, ad impressions, ad interactions, consent status, and diagnostic information for ad delivery, frequency capping, fraud prevention, measurement, and personalization where allowed.

If personalized advertising or tracking is used, iOS may show Apple's App Tracking Transparency permission prompt. You can manage tracking permissions in iOS Settings.

Notifications

Jackpot Lab may include optional draw reminder or notification features. If you enable notifications, Apple Push Notification service may process the device token needed to deliver notifications. Notification preferences may be stored locally on your device.

Analytics and Diagnostics

The current app is structured so analytics or crash reporting services can be added later. If Jackpot Lab adds analytics, crash reporting, or remote configuration services, this policy will be updated to describe the information processed by those services.

Third-Party Services

Jackpot Lab may use third-party services including Apple platform services and Google AdMob. These services may process information according to their own privacy policies and terms.

Data Deletion

You can delete locally stored Jackpot Lab data by deleting saved picks inside the app, clearing app data where supported, or uninstalling the app from your device. For privacy questions, contact pung0805@gmail.com.

Children's Privacy

Jackpot Lab is not directed to children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information to us, contact us so we can review and take appropriate action.

Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy when app features, advertising behavior, third-party services, legal requirements, or platform policies change. Updates will be posted on this page with a new effective date.
