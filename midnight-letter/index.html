English

Midnight Letter is an AI character conversation service for adults aged 18 or older, centered on emotionally supportive companions, reflection, romance, fantasy, and fictional stories.

Characters are not human, licensed counselors, or medical, legal, or financial professionals. Replies can be inaccurate or unexpected, and safety guidance takes priority over role-play in a crisis.
