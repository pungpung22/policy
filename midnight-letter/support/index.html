Support — English

We can help with guest accounts, AI chat and streaming, voice conversations, memories, usage, unsafe-reply reports, privacy requests, and account deletion.

What to include

  • Your country and app language.
  • Device, operating system, and app version.
  • Approximate time and steps to reproduce the issue.
  • A redacted screenshot or message/request ID.

Do not send passwords, authentication codes, card numbers, government identifiers, or complete conversation histories. Use the in-app Report action first for an unsafe AI reply.

United States crisis support

Call 911 for immediate danger. Call or text 988 for the Suicide & Crisis Lifeline. Midnight Letter does not replace these services.
